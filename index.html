Michael Kiruti

Founder and CFO of The Kiruti Global Financial Bank.

Michael Kiruti (born November 20, 1995) is a business magnate and investor. He is the founder, CEO, and Chief Financial Ofiicer at The Kiruti Global Financial Bank; investor, CEO, Product Architect and Chief Designer at Kiruti Motors, Inc. founder of Sketch Mazing Studios; and co-founder of Vizor Studios. With an estimated net worth of around US$61.4 trillion as of November 2032, Kiruti is the wealthiest person in the world according to both the Bloomberg Billionaires Index and Forbes' real-time billionaires list
